Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Overview

Our commitment to your privacy

Supaflash ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Data Collection

Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Name and email address when you sign in with Apple or Google
  • Display name if you choose to update it in your profile
  • Text, documents, or PDF content you submit to SupaAI for flash card generation
  • Prompts and topics you enter into the Ask SupaAI feature

Information collected automatically

  • Flash card study session results and card progress
  • Decks you download from the Supaflash catalog
  • Decks you create using SupaAI
  • XP earned and daily streak activity
  • AI generation usage count
AI Content

SupaAI and Content You Submit

When you use SupaAI features — including Ask SupaAI, Paste Notes, PDF, or Word document upload — the content you provide is sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. This content is used solely to generate flash cards or SupaRun games on your behalf.

We strongly advise against submitting personal, sensitive, confidential, or proprietary information through SupaAI features. Do not include passwords, financial data, health information, or personal details about yourself or others.

AI-generated content is processed by Google's Gemini API. Google's use of this data is governed by their own privacy policy and terms of service.

We do not use your submitted content to train AI models and we do not sell your content to third parties.

Usage

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the app, including your study sessions, flash card library, and progress tracking
  • Generate flash card decks and SupaRun games from content you submit via SupaAI
  • Track your XP, streaks, and mastery progress
  • Enforce AI generation usage limits (free tier vs Pro)
  • Serve and measure advertising to support the free operation of the app
  • Respond to support requests you send us

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third Parties

Third-Party Services

Supabase — We use Supabase to store your account data, deck library, and app activity. Supabase processes data on our behalf under their own privacy policy.

Google Gemini — Content you submit to SupaAI features is processed by Google's Gemini API to generate flash card decks and SupaRun games. Google's privacy policy governs their handling of this data.

Sign in with Apple / Google Sign-In — When you sign in using Apple or Google, we receive basic profile information (name and email) from those providers. Their respective privacy policies govern what data they collect during the sign-in process.

Google AdMob — Supaflash displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies, device identifiers, and similar technologies to serve relevant ads based on your interests and activity.

RevenueCat — We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscription entitlements. RevenueCat processes purchase data on our behalf under their own privacy policy.

Advertising

Advertising

Supaflash displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob to support free access to the app and its content.

On iOS, we request your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before allowing personalized advertising. If you decline, you will still see ads but they will not be personalized.

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you will be asked to provide consent before personalized ads are shown. You may withdraw or change your consent at any time by contacting us.

To learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use its services, visit: google.com/policies/privacy/partners

Account

Account Deletion & Data Removal

We offer two ways to delete your account and all associated data (including study progress, downloaded decks, created decks, XP, and streaks):

In-App Deletion

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This will immediately and permanently remove your data from our servers.

Email Request

If you no longer have the app installed, email us at joshua9park@gmail.com. Your request must come from the email address associated with your Apple or Google account used for Supaflash.

Once processed, all account data is permanently purged and cannot be recovered.

Local Storage

Data Stored on Your Device

Supaflash stores your downloaded flash card decks, user-created decks, and SupaRun games locally on your device for offline access. This data is cleared when you sign out or delete your account.

Children

Children's Privacy

Supaflash is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data (you can update your display name directly in your Profile)
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time via the methods described above
Updates

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Contact
